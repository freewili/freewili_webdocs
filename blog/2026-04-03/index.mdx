<br/>

## 💡 Inspiration

A more interactive way to control a robot.

---

## ⚙️ What It Does

Takes the X and Y position data of a FREE-WILi. Sends the data over rf, and then translates the changes into commands for the motor controllers to replace a traditional controller.

---

## 🛠️ How We Built It

FREE-WILi, C++ and WSL.

---

## ⚠️ Challenges We Ran Into

New technology and limited knowledge.

---

## 🏆 Accomplishments We're Proud Of

Gained exposure to new languages and embedded systems.

---

## 📚 What We Learned

The basics of embedded systems and radio communication.

---

## 🚀 What's next for SeanTimothyCollinsApp

Further work with cleaning radio messages and translating data points.

---

```c++``` ```free-wili``` ```wsl```

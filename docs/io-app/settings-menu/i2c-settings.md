# I2C settings

The I2C Settings are listed below:

| **Name**  	| **Description**                       	| **Notes**          	|
|-----------	|---------------------------------------	|--------------------	|
| Frequency 	| Bit rate of I2C bus                   	| Limited to 400Khz. 	|
| PullUps   	| Enable or disable 10K pull ups to VIO 	| Defaulted to On    	|
